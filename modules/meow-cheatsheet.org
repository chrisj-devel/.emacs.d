* Common Workflows

** Delete a word
=e= (mark word) then =d= (kill)
or: =w= (to next word) then =d=

** Delete a line
=v= (select line) then =d= (kill)

** Copy a line
=v= (select line) then =y= (copy)

** Change a word
=e= (mark word) then =c= (change)

** Select multiple lines
=v= then keep pressing =v= to expand

** Select inside parens
=,= then =r= (inner of round)

** Select around parens
=.= then =r= (bounds of round)

** Select inside function (tree-sitter)
=,= then =f= (inner of function)

** Select around class (tree-sitter)
=.= then =a= (bounds of class)
